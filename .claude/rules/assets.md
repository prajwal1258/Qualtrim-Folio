# Asset & Imagery Rules

## Required Imagery
- `pasta.jpg`: Fine pappardelle, close-up, dark wood background.
- `pizza.jpg`: Wood-fired pizza, top-down view.
- `burger.jpg`: Classic burger, side view.
- `dessert.jpg`: Strawberry parfait/mousse in glass jars.

## Optimization Guidelines
- Use WebP format where possible.
- Images must use `object-fit: cover` within their containers to maintain aspect ratio without distortion.
- Source from high-quality photography APIs (Unsplash) if local assets are unavailable.
